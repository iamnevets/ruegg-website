About the Firm

Rüegg & Associates is one of Switzerland's premier law firms, recognized for its exceptional legal services and commitment to excellence. We are ranked as a tier 1/top tier law firm in Switzerland by The Legal 500, specializing in Banking, Finance, M&A, Commercial, Corporate, Energy, Infrastructure, Projects, and Disputes. Chambers & Partners has shortlisted our firm as one of the "best law firms" in Switzerland.

Our firm is dedicated to providing top-notch legal services comparable to those offered by top-tier law firms in New York or London. We focus on serving foreign governments, global multinationals, and Fortune 500 companies doing business in Switzerland.

Additional Information

  • We are known for our responsiveness, solution-oriented approach, and ability to deliver quality work efficiently. Our 24-hour practice ensures that our clients' needs are always met.
  • Over 75% of our attorneys have tried jury cases to verdict, achieving success in some of the most significant cases in the country.
  • Our clientele includes a majority of the Fortune 500 companies doing business in the Netherlands, as well as over 100 global multinationals and leading companies in the oil, gas, and mining industries.
  • We are committed to maintaining our integrity and reputation for excellence. Our strict anti-bribery and anti-corruption policy ensures that we operate with the highest ethical standards.
