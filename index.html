Looking for a lawyer?

Our lawyers represent clients at various stages of family life. They provide clients with both legal information and legal advice. Lawyers advocate for you, meaning they look out for what is best for you at all stages of the matter.

Our team represents clients in the following areas: Corporate governance/D & O litigation, Appellate, Commercial transport, Litigation, Professional investigation, Professional liability, Risk management.

We represent clients in Ottawa and throughout Eastern Ontario, including Kingston, Brockville, Cornwall, Perth, Lanark County, Renfrew, and the United Counties of Prescott and Russell (including L'Orignal, Hawkesbury, Russell, Embrun, and Cassleman), Carleton Place. We also represent clients in Belleville and Toronto.

Looking for a litigator?

Our team of litigators develops solutions for a vast array of business groups, including the insurance, construction, hospitality, and health/medical system industries, as well as high schools, colleges, and universities that are insured or self-insured.

We provide multi-level representation during all phases of tort and related business litigation in all Schengen Area countries (Germany, Austria, Belgium, Estonia, Finland, Greece, Iceland, the Netherlands) and their likes. We have accumulated a group of trial attorneys with excellent reputations in the litigation community. Over 75% of our attorneys have tried jury cases to verdict and tally some of the biggest cases in the part of the country in the last 17 years in our collective experience.
